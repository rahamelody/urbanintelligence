FOOTPRiNT

  • Step 1

    Sign up for Footprint at a local business near you by using our retina scanner to authorize your identity. Click here to find the closest participating local business.

  • Step 2

    Once your identity is validated and you have provided your email address, you will get an email in your inbox with a link to activate your dashboard.

  • Step 3

    Begin racking up seeeds by attending community-driven events in your area aimed at decreasing our carbon footprint. You can rack up extra points by updating and tracking your utility bills as well.

  • Step 4

    Trade in your seeeds for more autonomy over your digital data. Control who has access to your bank account info, the people you talk to, the things you like, the things you buy, etc. Destroy, Retract, or Transfer this data whenever you like.
